CTL (Chronic Training Load / Fitness): 42-day EMA of daily load — rises with consistent, sustained training. ATL (Acute Training Load / Fatigue): 7-day EMA — spikes after hard weeks, drops quickly with rest. TSB (Training Stress Balance / Form) = CTL − ATL; positive = fresh & ready to race, negative = under accumulated fatigue. Daily load = weekly Relative Effort ÷ 7. Model uses weekly exponential decay (CTL α = 0.1573/wk via 42-day constant; ATL α = 0.6601/wk via 7-day constant).
