Fitness & Freshness (CTL / ATL / TSB)

CTL (Chronic Training Load) = 42-day exponential moving average of daily load — represents fitness base.  ATL (Acute Training Load) = 7-day EMA — recent fatigue.  TSB (Training Stress Balance / Form) = CTL − ATL. Positive = fresh & rested; negative = accumulated fatigue. Daily load = weekly Relative Effort ÷ 7. EMA decay per week: CTL × 0.8427 + load × 0.1573; ATL × 0.3399 + load × 0.6601.
